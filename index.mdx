Save Sora is an open-source Chrome extension built for one purpose: helping you back up your own Sora videos before they're gone. It connects to your existing logged-in Sora session, builds a local review list inside the popup, and lets you search, sort, rename, and selectively download exactly what you want — packaged neatly into a ZIP archive or saved as individual files.

## What Save Sora does

Save Sora opens an inactive Sora tab in Chrome, injects its own packaged code into that tab, reads your library through your existing signed-in session, and presents everything in a popup dashboard. Nothing leaves your browser — no tokens, no cookies, no server uploads.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the extension">
    Download the repository as a ZIP, unzip it, open `chrome://extensions`, enable **Developer mode**, and click **Load unpacked** to select the folder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in to Sora">
    Open [sora.chatgpt.com](https://sora.chatgpt.com) in the same Chrome profile and sign in normally. Save Sora uses your existing session — no passwords or tokens to paste.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the popup and fetch">
    Click the Save Sora extension icon, choose your sources (Published, Drafts, Likes, etc.), and click **Fetch Videos**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review, rename, and download">
    Browse the list, rename anything you want, check your selections, and click **Download Videos** to save your archive.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Save Sora is designed for desktop Chrome. It does not work on mobile browsers or other Chromium-based browsers that don't support Manifest V3 extensions with the required permissions.
</Note>
